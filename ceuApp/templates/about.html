About Us


California licensed Acupuncturists are required to receive Continuing Education to renew their license every two years. We provide the highest quality courses to help you meet this requirement. We offer classes in Chinese Herbs, Acupuncture and many other related subjects.


Our Mission


We offer high quality and cost-effective continuing education to enable TCM practitioners to gain holistic evidence-based alternative medicine healing techniques.


Our Vision


Each patient you have served will become intrinsically appreciate alternative medicine natural healing benefits while desperately wanting to promote alternative medicine to help to enhance family or community overall wellbeing.
